Powerful Predator

Lions are among the strongest predators on Earth. Their teamwork, powerful roar, and hunting skills make them one of Africa's most respected animals.

A lion's roar can be heard from nearly 8 kilometers away. They spend most of their day resting and usually hunt during the cooler hours of the day.

The Lion's Mane

The magnificent mane is one of the lion's most iconic features. It protects the neck during fights and signals strength.

The darker and thicker the mane, the healthier and stronger the lion is often considered by other lions.

Life of Lionesses

Lionesses are the primary hunters of the pride and work together using incredible teamwork to capture prey.

They communicate using body language and soft vocal sounds while coordinating group hunts.

Amazing Facts

  • 🦁 Lions live in groups called prides.
  • 🌍 Most wild lions are found in Africa.
  • 🏃 Lions can run nearly 80 km/h for short distances.
  • 😴 They sleep around 20 hours every day.
  • 🔊 Their roar can travel up to 8 km.
